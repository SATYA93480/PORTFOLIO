Vision & Professional Journey

Born on 19th September 1994, I am an IT entrepreneur from Odisha and the Managing Director of SonyDig Technology Private Limited. After completing my Diploma, B.Tech (2017), and MBA, I founded SonyDig in 2019 with a clear vision — to drive digital transformation through AI-powered innovation, cybersecurity excellence, and technology-led growth.

I specialize in AI & Automation, Cybersecurity, Business Strategy, Branding, Research, and Project Management. Over the years, I have led a 50+ member team and successfully delivered 100+ projects across government, corporate, and private sectors.

Beyond business, I am deeply committed to social impact. Since 2016, I have been serving as the Secretary of Sarba Shakti Educational Charitable Trust, where I lead initiatives focused on women and child safety, legal awareness, digital literacy, self-defense training, and entrepreneurship development programs that promote financial independence.

I also founded Jagannatha Krupa, a cultural e-commerce platform dedicated to promoting Puri’s heritage products and supporting local artisans. My work includes executing major digital initiatives for the Odisha Culture Department, developing AI-powered e-Office automation systems, and implementing the OCHIP program to accelerate digital transformation and skill development.

My contributions to women empowerment and technology innovation have been recognized with several prestigious awards:

  • Biju Kanya Ratna Award
  • Udyog Urja Award (2023)
  • Star Alumni Award (2022)
  • Shakti Award (2022)
  • Cyber Award (2023)

I am empaneled with OCAC, NACOF, and OSIC, further strengthening my engagement with institutional and government projects.
