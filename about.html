Vision & Professional Journey

Based in Odisha, I lead SonyDig Technology Private Limited as Managing Director, shaping digital strategy across AI-driven innovation, cybersecurity, and brand transformation. My academic journey includes a Diploma, B.Tech (2017), and MBA, which laid the foundation for launching SonyDig in 2019.

Today, my work spans government, corporate, and private sectors, delivering end-to-end solutions in automation, security, research, and digital growth. I focus on building systems that are practical, scalable, and rooted in real community needs.

Social impact remains central to my leadership. As Secretary of Sarba Shakti Educational Charitable Trust since 2016, I guide programs for women and child safety, legal awareness, digital literacy, self-defense, and entrepreneurship development.

I also founded Jagannatha Krupa, a cultural e-commerce platform elevating Puri?s heritage products and supporting local artisans. My recent work includes cultural digitization and enterprise automation programs designed to accelerate digital adoption.
